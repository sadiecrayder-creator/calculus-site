AP Calculus AB: Tangent Lines and Derivatives

In AP Calculus, the derivative of a function at a point represents the slope of the tangent line to the graph at that point.

In this visualization, we explore the function:

f(x) = x²

The derivative of this function is:

f′(x) = 2x
